# Seaview Cottage — Booking Policy & Guest FAQ

## Check-in and check-out
Check-in is from 4pm. Early check-in from 1pm is possible for £25 if the
previous guest has already left — ask us on the day of arrival. Checkout is
10am. Late checkout until 12pm is £20 when available.

## Pets
We allow well-behaved dogs — maximum 2 — for a £30 cleaning fee per stay.
No other pets. Assistance dogs stay free of charge.

## Parties and events
No stag or hen parties, and no events. The cottage sleeps 6; the maximum
overnight occupancy is 6 guests.

## Payment and cancellation
A 25% deposit secures the booking; the balance is due 14 days before arrival.
Free cancellation up to 30 days before arrival. Within 30 days the deposit is
non-refundable; within 7 days the full amount is charged.

## Amenities
Free WiFi throughout. A travel cot and high chair are available on request at
no charge. The kitchen is fully equipped. There is one allocated parking space.
